Holistic Coaching



Admissions is holistic, so why shouldn’t coaching be too? At Ivy Map, we believe that helping you succeed as a student in every way is the best way to stack the deck in your favor. We offer 1-on-1 Zoom coaching for as long as you need to craft an extraordinary application.

Our Services

Our college consultancy model sees our students receive individualised support in every aspect of their US application — from university selection strategy, test prep and personal statement/essay support, to extracurricular mentoring and interview practice. Here's how we work!

Write mature and compelling essays.

We’ll provide techniques you can use to not only ideate and write sophisticated essays, but to tell your personal story.

Flourish in examinations and tests.

Our coaches double-up as academic tutors and can teach you the correct methods to supercharge your learning.

Building ECAs & Portfolio

We’ll show you how to sell yourself using the extracurricular and additional information sections of the Common App.

Connect with experienced applicants.

Our team is ideally situated to assist with interview preparation and teacher recommendations.
